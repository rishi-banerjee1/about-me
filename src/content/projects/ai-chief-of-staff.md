## The problem

Important work is often buried under message volume. Slack, Gmail, and calendar invites all act like separate inboxes, so triage becomes a daily tax instead of a system.

## What I built

AI Chief of Staff reads across those sources, classifies the work by urgency and action type, drafts responses where useful, and keeps a ledger of what is still open versus actually resolved.

## Why it matters

What makes this interesting is not just the agent label. It is the operational shape: scheduled automations, durable state, feedback loops, and a bias toward closing loops instead of producing disposable summaries.
